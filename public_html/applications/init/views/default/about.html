Welcome to Fitness Challenge!


Fitness Challenge is an online community where participants unite for the common goal of wellness.

Using competition and comradery as driving forces, members set personal fitness goals while utilizing the support and inspiration that flourishes in community settings.

Fitness Challenge is free to use and, while competitive in nature, the Challenges are intended as a fun way to encourage healthy activity.
