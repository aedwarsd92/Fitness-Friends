Welcome to Fitness Challenge!


Fitness Challenge is an online community where participants unite for the common goal of wellness.

Using competition and comradery as driving forces, members set personal fitness goals while utilizing the support and inspiration that flourishes in community settings.

Fitness Challenge is free to use and, while competitive in nature, the Challenges are intended as a fun way to encourage healthy activity.

Challenge Descriptions

Challenge Name:

This is the first thing people will see about your Challenge. It can be the name of your group, a specific event, or anythig you want. For example: Another Kick Butt Challenge!

Describe Your Challenge:

Tell everyone what this challenge is about and why they should join. Is it meant for weight loss, or number of miles run, or number of steps taken?

Start Date and End Date:

This will tell people when the Challenge begins and when it ends.

How often should participants record their status:

This tells people how often to record their progress on the challenge. You can select Daily, Weekly, or Monthly. For example, if the Challenge has a Start Date of Saturday November 5th, and set to Weekly, Participants can enter their progress once a week on Saturday.

Metric Name :

This tells people specifically what the Challenge measures to determine a winner. For example, laps in the pool, or fastest 5k, or situps.

Metric Type :

This is the category of the Challenges metric. It can be set to categories like Repetitions, Sets, Miles, Time, BMI, etc.
